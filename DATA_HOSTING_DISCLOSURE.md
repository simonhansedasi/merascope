# Data Hosting & Residency Disclosure

**Effective date:** 2026-06-25

## Where your data is stored

Merascope is hosted on servers located in **Helsinki, Finland** (European Union), operated by Hetzner Online GmbH. All data submitted through the platform — including study inputs, evidence records, and site assessments — is stored and processed on these servers.

## What data we collect

Merascope collects the following information from users:

- Email address (used for account identification and steward commission)
- Site assessment inputs you submit (location, study type, indicators selected)
- Evidence records and findings you author

We do not collect personally identifiable information beyond your email address. We do not collect payment information, government credentials, or internal agency documents.

## Implications for government users

Merascope is **not currently FedRAMP authorized**. Federal employees subject to FedRAMP requirements should consult their agency IT or security office before using this service for official work.

State and local government employees are generally not subject to FedRAMP. However, if your agency has data residency requirements restricting use of EU-hosted services, please review those policies before submitting official work product.

If your agency requires a vendor agreement, data processing addendum, or hosting certification before onboarding, contact us at woses21@gmail.com.

## Data transfer

By using Merascope, you acknowledge that your data will be transferred to and stored in Finland. If this is incompatible with your organization's policies, do not submit work product through the platform.
